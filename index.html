A. P. J. Abdul Kalam

About The Legend

☛ Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, the "Missile Man of India," was a distinguished scientist renowned for his pivotal role in India's missile and nuclear programs.

☛ Serving as India's 11th President from 2002 to 2007, Dr. Kalam inspired the nation with his vision for a developed India by 2020.

☛ An eloquent writer autobiography "Wings of Fire" documents his journey from a newspaper vendor to a revered scientist.

☛ Dr. Kalam's engagement with youth earned him the title of "People's President," motivating generations to pursue excellence.

☛ His leadership during the successful Pokhran-II nuclear tests in 1998 established India as a formidable nuclear power.
